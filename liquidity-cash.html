Stablecoin Labs is a registered US company that has been operating on the market since 2019. We successfully serviced thousands of market makers and liquidity providers and continue to provide a safe way to earn passive income holding and exchanging stablecoins from their personal wallets. Our dedicated Support team stands guard 24/7 to help you with any exchange-related questions that might arise.

Stake stablecoins in your wallet and proceed to rebalancing operations when required to earn daily rewards. Rebalancing operations consist in receiving onto your exchange account tokens from one chain and send tokens in return from your wallet on another chain. Maximum monthly value to exchange do not exceed staked value.

As long as you keep your staked tokens in your wallet, you will continue to receive daily rewards. You can withdraw rewards or staked tokens any time without limitation.

When you decide to withdraw tokens from your wallet. Daily rewards stop from being perceived.
